Video Diffusion Models — Breakthroughs (2024)

1. Sora (OpenAI) — Treats video as spacetime patches, enabling world-simulation at any resolution. Trained on diverse durations without recaptioning. Key innovation: unified images + video in one training regime.

2. Lumiere (Google Research) — Space-Time U-Net generates the full video duration in a single pass (no keyframe interpolation), producing globally coherent motion free of flickering.

3. CogVideoX (Zhipu AI/Tsinghua) — Open-source, 3D full-attention transformer. Strong prompt adherence. Available free via diffusers.

Python — load CogVideoX:
from diffusers import CogVideoXPipeline
import torch

pipe = CogVideoXPipeline.from_pretrained("THUDM/CogVideoX-5b", torch_dtype=torch.bfloat16).to("cuda")
video = pipe(prompt="A drone over misty mountains at golden hour", num_frames=49).frames[0]

from diffusers.utils import export_to_video
export_to_video(video, "output.mp4", fps=8)
